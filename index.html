About

Alex Mercer

I'm a photographer based in the Pacific Northwest with a passion for capturing the quiet moments where light and life intersect. My work spans intimate portraiture and sweeping landscapes — two disciplines that, to me, ask the same question: what does it feel like to be here?

With over a decade behind the lens, I've worked with editorial clients, couples, and brands who value images that breathe. When I'm not shooting, you'll find me somewhere in the mountains with a film camera and too much coffee.
